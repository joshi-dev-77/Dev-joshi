  1. KATA :

    • KATA means "FORMS" in japanese language , describing detailed pattterns of movements practiced. karate kata are executed as a specific series of variety of moves , with stepping and turning , while attempting to maintain perfect form.

    • The regular practice of KATA is essential as it develops the proper body mechanic , help builds muscle memory , develops mindfullness and favors the adequate execution of breathing , but its purpose is actually the core of karate

  2. KUMITE :

    • KUMITE means "GRAPPLING HANDS" and is one of the three main section of karate training , along with kata and kihon.kumite is the part of karate in which a person trains againts an adversary , using the techniques learned form the kihon and kata .
